What I Do

  • I scrape websites using Python, use R for Data Analysis and Tableau for Visualization.
  • I develop skills for Amazon's Alexa using Flask-Ask.
  • I make music videos for my friend's YouTube Channel.
  • I'm currently the Media and IT Vice Chair for Sigma Alpha Delta. Most of my work involves photography and cinematography of Sigma's workshops and professional events. During the Fall 2016 semester, I co-led a photoshop and a photography workshop which were attended by at least 50 students. I also built a prototype of an Attendance web application to assist them on taking attendance of every student who are attending the event. The application was built using Python's Django Framework.
